Interests

I am a passionate developer working on solving 2D and 3D computer vision problems using the latest scientific break-through. I am particularly interest in medical applications (mini-invasive surgery, pediatric care, oncology) and robotic systems.

My research interests include: artificial intelligence, neural networks, structures and shapes recognition, 3D geometry and modeling, fuzzy logic, spatial reasoning, decision theory, machine learning, medical imaging and clinical data analysis.

Projects

I am involved in a variety of projects in research labs and start-up environments. I am mainly based at IMAG2 Laboratory on the Necker campus. I follow data science projects at Imagine Institute and I am part of the Bio-Entrepreneurs program. I participate in the research activities of the LTCI Lab @ Télécom Paris since 2018 and I am active in the innovation, entrepreneurship and start-up environments. I regularly give lectures on medical imaging for Master programs and I mentor engineering and medicine students during internships.
