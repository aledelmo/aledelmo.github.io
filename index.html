Let me introduce myself.

Hello everyone! I am Alessandro and I solve problems.

Who am I?

Born in Turin, Italy. I got my engineering diploma there before moving to Paris to work. I started doing research right after finishing my studies, always keeping a developer attitude. I am currently the engineer of the IMAG2 team, a group composed by surgeons and PhD students, working on the 3D modeling of the pelvic anatomical structures.
