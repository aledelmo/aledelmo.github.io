Hello, I am

Alessandro Delmonte

Artificial Intelligence & Computer Vision Engineer

Biomedical engineer specialized in artificial intelligence, machine learning and computer vision with applications to medical imaging.

I am Senior Artificial Intelligence Engineer at the Clario R&D medical imaging division, working on deep learning technologies for medical images analysis in the context of large-scale clinical trials.
In 2017, I graduated in Biomedical Engineering (equiv. MSc) from Polytechnic University of Turin, Italy, while part of the image and signal processing laboratory of Télécom Paris, France. In 2018, I joined the image-guided surgery team of Necker Hospital, leading the AI research activity and managing the tech transfer operations. The developed methods were used in more than 100 complex operations, the project entering the ParisBiotechSanté start-up incubator in 2021. In 2022, I joined Clario for my current position as deep-learning engineer.
My interests include supervised learning, neural networks, medical imaging, shapes recognition, decision theory, explainable AI and robotics. I authored multiple peer-reviewed publications on AI methods for MRI processing and I am the main contributor to various imaging software solutions.
I am active in the entrepreneurship and start-up environments, participating in the HEC Bio-Entrepreneurs program and co-founding a start-up spin-off of the Imagine Institute. With my industry, academic and public healthcare experiences, I regularly give lectures on medical imaging for Master programs, and I mentor junior engineers and medicine students.
